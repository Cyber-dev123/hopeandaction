About Us

We believe real life is
the greatest adventure

Hope And Action was founded on a simple truth: children and families thrive when they put down their devices and engage with the world around them. From outdoor play to face-to-face conversation, real connection matters.

We run workshops, community events, and campaigns to help families set healthy boundaries with technology — and rediscover how much fun life can be without a screen in hand.

💡 Tips & Tricks

Fun things to do instead of screens

Struggling to put down the phone? Here are some easy, fun ideas for kids and families to try today!

🌿

Go on a nature scavenger hunt

Make a list of things to find outside — a pinecone, a red leaf, a bug, a cloud shaped like an animal. Race to find them all!

📚

Start a family book club

Pick a book everyone reads together. Chat about it over dinner — kids love sharing opinions and it builds great conversation skills!

🎨

Get creative with art

Grab some paper and pencils and draw, paint, or make collages. No talent needed — just imagination and fun!

🍳

Cook something together

Let kids help make a meal or bake cookies. Cooking teaches real life skills and tastes delicious — win win!

🎲

Play a board game

Dust off those board games and get competitive! Games like Uno, Jenga, or Scrabble bring the whole family together.

🚴

Explore your neighbourhood

Go for a bike ride or walk and discover places you've never noticed before. You'd be surprised what's right around the corner!
